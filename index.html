Our Story
Our entrepreneurial heritage anchors every decision we make.

BE Capital is built on the success of our founder, Stéphane Bonvin. In 1994 he launched Investis Group, spotting a distinctive opportunity in the Swiss real estate market. What began as a disciplined focus on residential properties around the Lake Geneva region grew into one of Switzerland’s leading listed real estate companies.

Over three decades, a clear entrepreneurial spirit, deep market knowledge, and an unwavering buy-and-hold strategy generated enduring value. The 2016 listing of Investis on the SIX Swiss Exchange is the foundation upon which our family office stands.

Our Mission

Created in 2015, BE Capital SA manages the Bonvin family’s assets and extends our entrepreneurial DNA into new arenas. While our roots are firmly grounded in real estate, our mandate reaches further.
